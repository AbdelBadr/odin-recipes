Smoked Corn on Cob

Delicious corn-on-cob does wonder on a sunny summer day.

Corns are nutritious and can be cooked in various ways. My favourite way of cooking corn is by making those delicious corn-on-cob. My children love them, they would pack extras to school to give to their friends.

Some people may be intimated by the recipe, but if the instructions are followed exactly you will end up with the best cooking experience ever.

Ingredients

  • 4 ears corn on the cob, silks removed/li>
  • mesquite wood chips
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 lime, zested
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

    Step 1

  1. Place corn back in their husks in a large pot of water and soak for 2 hours. Soak wood chips in another container of water for about 30 minutes.

    2. Step 2

  2. Preheat a smoker to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C). Place corn, unstacked, onto wire racks. Place the racks into the smoker. Add wood chips according to the manufacturer's directions. Smoke until corn reaches your desired level of tenderness, 60 to 75 minutes.

    3. Step 3

  3. Combine butter, cilantro, lime zest, lime juice, and paprika in a small bowl. Peel back the husks and brush corn with butter mixture. Season with salt and pepper.
